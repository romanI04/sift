Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

What we collect

When you join our waitlist, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The newsletter URL you provide
  • Your approximate location (country, derived from your IP address)
  • The date you signed up

How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Set up a search page for your newsletter
  • Email you when your search page is ready
  • Send occasional product updates (you can unsubscribe anytime)

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data to anyone
  • We don't share your email with third parties
  • We don't use tracking cookies on our site

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on Cloudflare Workers KV, a secure cloud storage service operated by Cloudflare, Inc. Data is encrypted in transit.

Search data

When you use a Sift search page, all search processing happens in your browser. Your search queries are never sent to our servers.

Your rights

You can request to see, update, or delete your data at any time by emailing us. If you're in the EU, you have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to object to processing.
